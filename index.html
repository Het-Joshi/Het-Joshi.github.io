Introduction — the conviction

True engineering doesn't happen in a textbook; it happens when you break a system down to its silicon and rebuild it better. That conviction is what turned a handful of weekend hackathons into Mukti — one of the largest student Free and Open Source Software communities in India, a network of builders shipping real code and reclaiming their digital autonomy.

"I am a man, and all that affects mankind concerns me." — Bhagat Singh

The mission has never changed: democratize the kind of engineering knowledge you don't get from a lecture, but earn by taking things apart. The work below is that mission applied to the systems most people are forced to trust without ever being asked.

Affiliations & trajectory

Northeastern University — Mon(IoT)r Lab

2025 — present · Boston
graduate research apprentice · adv. Prof. David Choffnes

Dissecting blackbox automatic content recognition (ACR) across twelve retail smart TVs spanning eight operating systems — mapping exactly how the firmware fingerprints the people who buy them, and whether opt-out switches do anything at all.

IIT Bombay — Trust Lab

May 2024 — May 2025 · Mumbai
security researcher · adv. Prof. Devashish Gosain

First empirical measurement of tunnel-based reverse-proxy services (Ngrok, Cloudflared, and peers) across distributed vantage points — mapping their hidden topology, privacy implications, and viability as Tor relays. Lead author on the resulting study.

ISRO — Indian Space Research Organisation

Apr — Jun 2024 · Bengaluru
systems & software security intern

Designed and hardened a communication protocol for LIDAR sensor data on embedded aerospace hardware. When failure means losing a satellite's eyes, memory safety and a verified secure-boot chain are not optional.

Ramaiah Institute of Technology

Oct 2024 — Jun 2025 · Bangalore
teaching assistant · network security

Lectured 120+ students on Network Security and Data Communication Networks — ARP poisoning, IPv6 MITM, Snort IDS, SambaCry — with labs that made them earn the concept by breaking it first. To secure a system is to understand it well enough to teach it.

Selected systems

sys-01 · measurement

Distributed 20-node platform

python · ~8K LOC · 2+ TB/day

The measurement backbone behind the tunnel and reverse-proxy studies — vantage points, orchestration, and a parallel collection pipeline.

sys-02 · agent security

AI Agent Security Mesh

mcp-aware reverse proxy · semantic DLP

A reverse proxy that understands the Model Context Protocol and applies semantic data-loss prevention between an agent and the tools it can reach.

sys-03 · agent security

WhisperBridge

tor-routed local LLM · PII anonymization

Multi-agent indirect-prompt-injection defense that treats all fetched web content as an untrusted threat vector before it ever reaches the model.

sys-04 · cloud security

EgressProbe

go · kubernetes egress auditor

Audits Kubernetes egress policy — what a workload can actually reach versus what its network policy claims to allow.

sys-05 · tooling

paperlab

fastapi · ollama · fully local

A local AI research workbench for reviewing measurement papers — reviewer personas, a fact-check pipeline, and self-hosted search, all on a 16GB laptop.

sys-06 · covert channels

SSHRead Under review

channel where none should exist

A covert channel riding a service deployed across most of the internet — no session, no data plane, no signature. Mechanism withheld pending peer review.

sys-07 · ev security

Kavaach

ocpp testbed · python · rpi

The de-facto OCPP testbed replicating real EV charging stations — SYN floods, billing exploits, and kernel-level mitigation benchmarks.

sys-08 · wifi security

Sanjay

cli · scapy

Detects malicious or insecure Wi-Fi — Evil Twin, Honeypot, and Deauth attacks — through active and passive scanning.
